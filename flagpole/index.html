Help restore the Weldon Circle Flagpole to its former glory! Our beloved flagpole has become weathered and rusted and pitted throughout. Lead-based paint is flaking off the flagpole onto the surrounding sidewalk and grass.

All contributions will directly fund the restoration project which includes:

  • Removal and abatement of existing lead-based paint
  • Repainting the historic flagpole
  • Upgrading weathered pulleys and ropes
  • All labor and materials (including bringing temporary power and water to the site)
