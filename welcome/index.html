Medfield Community Assocation

The Medfield Community Assocation would like to extend a warm welcome to you and your family.

We hope you love it here as much as we do. Medfield is a close-knit, residential neighborhood, but we also enjoy our proximity to shops and restaurants in nearby communities.

The Medfield Community Association (MCA) is a volunteer group that empowers our community to improve our quality of life through communication, engagement, participation, and advocacy. We would love to meet you in person, and invite you to join us at our next MCA meeting or event:

Our monthly meetings are held the second Tuesday of every month at 7pm at Medfield Heights Elementary School Community Room, 4300 Buchanan Avenue, Baltimore, MD 21211

These meetings are hybrid, with the option to participate via Zoom. Click here to access the meeting link.
