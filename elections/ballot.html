Medfield Community Association

Budget approval and Board of Directors election for fiscal year beginning July 1, 2022

The MCA will hold our annual Board of Directors election and budget approval vote remotely, via the same process we used last year.

The voting period is May 31 through June 14, 2022.

MCA members may submit their vote in one of two ways: an electronic ballot or a paper ballot.

Electronic ballot

If you would like to vote via the electronic ballot, simply click the button below during the voting period. Links to the relevant reference materials are available from within the ballot. If you are not yet an MCA member, you can find a link to join on the front page of this site.

Open Electronic Ballot

Paper ballot

A tear-out ballot is included in the June 2022 edition of the Medfielder newsletter. Due to space limitations, only a summary of the budget was included in the newsletter. If you wish to view the full proposed budget, that is available here:

View Proposed FY2023 Budget

If you haven't received a copy of the Medfielder on your doorstep, you can find it at one of Medfield's four Little Free Libraries or download and print it here.

Paper ballots may be returned via two methods:

  • USPS: mail to Medfield Community Association, PO Box 4782, Baltimore, MD, 21211
  • Ballot drop box: deposit in container labeled “MCA Ballots” on the front porch at 4404 Buchanan Avenue

If you are not yet an MCA member, you can join now by including a check with your ballot. Memberships are $10 for a household, $5 for senior citizens. Please ensure your check includes your Medfield address.
