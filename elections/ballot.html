Budget approval and Board of Directors election for fiscal year beginning July 1, 2020

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic and cancellation of in-person meetings for the foreseeable future, the MCA will hold our annual Board of Directors election and budget approval vote remotely.

Ways you can vote:

Paper ballot

If you already have a paper ballot and want to view a copy of the proposed budget, you may download it here:

Download budget.

A tear-out ballot was included in the June 2020 edition of the Medfielder newsletter. If you haven't received one on your doorstep, you may download and print a copy here:

Download a paper ballot.

If you lack access to a printer, extra copies of the newsletter are available at one of the three Little Free Library locations in Medfield.

Ballots may be returned via two methods:

  • USPS: mail to Medfield Community Associations, PO Box 4782, Baltimore, MD, 21211
  • Ballot drop box: deposit in container labeled "MCA Ballots" on the front porch at 1315 W. 40th Street

Electronic ballot

You may also vote electronically via the embedded from below. If you have trouble with the embedded form, you may open the ballot in a separate window here:
