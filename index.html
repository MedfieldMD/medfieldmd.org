Medfield Community Association

The Medfield Community Association (MCA) is a volunteer group that empowers our community to improve our quality of life through communication, engagement, participation, and advocacy in Baltimore, Maryland.

Support the MCA

Support our efforts in the neighborhood by joining the MCA or making a donation. Annual household dues are $10 ($5 for seniors) and renew on July 1. Please include your address and the names of everyone included in your membership when renewing online. For targeted donations (e.g. Weldon Circle flag/flagpole), please indicate this in the notes. Thank you!
